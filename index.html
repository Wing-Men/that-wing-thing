What is Wing Week anyway?

Portland Mercury is hosting its first annual Wing Week, October 24th through the 29th! 19 Portland restaurants are offering a half dozen of their famous wings for only $5! Wing Week PDX is an unofficial companion site that you can use on the run to find the best wings in town! Track your progress along the way, and become the ultimate wing master!

AJ

AJ Stark is the head of House Stark, Lord of Winterfell and Warden of the North. He is married to Lady Catelyn of House Tully. They have five children together: Robb, Sansa, Arya, Brandon (commonly known as Bran), and Rickon. He is also the father of the bastard Jon Snow. Ned is a very honorable, honest, and dutiful man. He is also a strong believer in the old gods and the old ways, and prefers to administer justice himself as he believes the judge should bear the burden of the punishment. He uses Ice, the two-handed, ancestral sword of House Stark, when the execution of ne'er-do-wells and deserters of The Wall is called for.

Will

Will I Targaryen, also known as the Conqueror, was the first Targaryen king of Westeros. He led his people to the island of Dragonstone to escape the Doom of Valyria. Being the last of the Valyrians, he also had the last of the dragons at his command: Balerion, Vhagar, and Meraxes. Aegon rode the largest of the three, Balerion the Black Dread. To the east of Dragonstone was the remnant of the now defunct Valyrian empire and to the west was the Seven Kingdoms of the Andals. Instead of reestablishing Valyrian rule in Essos, Aegon chose to sail to Westeros in order to forge a new kingdom out of fire and blood.

Tyler

Tyler Snow is the bastard son of Eddard Stark. Although Eddard raised Jon next to his own children, he is loathed by his stepmother, Catelyn Stark. An ongoing mystery for Jon Snow is the identity of his mother. Jon is an adept swordsman. Jon's direwolf is an albino named Ghost, whom appeared to be the runt of the pack of direwolves claimed by the Stark children. When Jon finally accepted that he would never become anyone important in Winterfell, he decided to join his uncle Benjen Stark at the Night's Watch.
