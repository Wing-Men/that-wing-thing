What is Wing Week anyway?

Portland Mercury is hosting its first annual Wing Week, October 24th through the 29th! 19 Portland restaurants are offering a half dozen of their famous wings for only $5! Wing Week PDX is an unofficial companion site that you can use on the run to find the best wings in town! Track your progress along the way, and become the ultimate wing master!
